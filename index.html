01 · The Problem

Every morning,
Dakar bleeds time.

Dakar is a city of 4 million people on a 550 km² peninsula. Twice a day it grinds to a halt. Commuters lose hours, miss jobs, lose income — and Senegal loses $1.4 billion every year to congestion alone.

The apps that could help? They're in French. They demand literacy. They weren't built for the people who need them most — the 80% who rely on informal transport and the 46% who don't read French.

04 · AI & Accessibility

Built for
everyone.

🎙️

Wolof ASR Engine

Powered by open-source Wolof speech recognition from Baamtu Datamation + Google WAXAL. Understands accents, slang, local place names.

🖼️

Icon-Only Navigation

Full icon-driven UI requiring zero text. Tap a bus — find buses. Tap a car — find a cab. Designed for bright sunlight on a moving vehicle.

📱

JAKKAL Lite (USSD)

Dial *707# for Wolof SMS with traffic status and next bus time. Works on any phone, any carrier. Zero data needed.

🧠

AI Traffic Prediction

ML model trained on Dakar patterns — prayer times, market days, school hours. Predicts congestion before it happens.

🌍

Community Crowdsourcing

Users report accidents, floods, broken lights. Each report earns mobile data credits — turning commuters into contributors.
